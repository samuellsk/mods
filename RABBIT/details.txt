"Rarasek"
"A mini keeper based upon the comic / manhwa labyrinth odyssey - the festival of delight , dreamlike joy and madness.

Features
2 different keepers for male and female.
2 different workers for male and female.
Acrobats , clowns , jugglers and minstrels.
Custom adamantium items and spells.

A mini intro and tooltips to help you understand how mechanics do work.

Core mechanic revolves around killing. The more your minions kill the stronger will they become. Even the newly summoned as these kills
are collective kills towards the global pool that unlocks stats improving mechanics and buffs.

If you have any suggestion come to the keeperrl discord.

Thank you.
